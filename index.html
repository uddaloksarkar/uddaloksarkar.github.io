Uddalok Sarkar

Hi! I'm a PhD student of Computer Science at Indian Statistical Institute. I am fortunate to be advised by Sourav Chakraborty and Kuldeep Meel. I am a part of Meelgroup.

My current research interest lies in the intersection of distribution testing and formal verification. I look into applying theory of distribution testing towards building robust verifiers for testing real-world distributions, like, samplers or generative models. Broadly, I am interested in problems that lie in the intersection of theory and practice.

My research has been recognized and supported by the Google PhD Fellowship (2023–2027).
